# Jai Samadhi

Jai Samadhi is a spiritual platform dedicated to the **Baba Garib Das Temple** in Kashmore (Ghouspur), Pakistan. Established approximately 200 years ago, this sacred sanctuary serves as a beacon of faith, hope, and healing for devotees worldwide.

- **Saints & Preachings**: Learn about the saints and their spiritual contributions.
- **Bhajan Collection**: Access a curated collection of spiritual songs (Bhajans).
- **AI Spiritual Guide**: An integrated AI chatbot to assist with spiritual queries and provide information about the temple's history.
- **Mobile Experience**: Easy access to spiritual content on the go with our mobile-friendly interface.
- **Community Connection**: Direct links to official social media channels and WhatsApp communities.
